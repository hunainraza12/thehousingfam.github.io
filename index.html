OUR CARBON FOOTPRINT

  • We believe that everyone shares equal responsibility towards the welfare of our planet, and work tirelessly to support the growth of mindset and knowledge in regards to environmental awareness, specifically, with in inner-city communities.

  • The Housing Fam take great pride in educating the younger generation towards their individual carbon footprint, and the simple steps they can take to reduce such.

  • We choose to lead by example, powering our premises with eco-energy where at all possible reducing our reliance upon fossil fuels, whilst opting for solar efficiency instead.

  • We're big on recycling. Our waste food is used for compost to feed vegetation, reducing our contribution towards landfills

  • We've installed sensory water dispensers and light sensors to prevent over usage and waste.

  • Many of our premises are equipped with eco-transportation.

  • We've broke up with paper as we're trying to be as digital as possible now. But bear with us, as we're not perfect.
